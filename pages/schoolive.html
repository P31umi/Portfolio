schoolive

Client

Schoolive is a School Management web application. It served different users based on roles assigned to them. For example, students could check their results and timetables, while teachers can upload student records and update student attendace records.

Task

My task was to simplify the whole experience. I divided my research and design process into the following steps:

Intercepts & Interviews

Through my intercepts and interviews, i realised a few things users found difficult:
- Data upload was a pain (specifically excel and csv files)
- No preview of data before upload
- Poor calendar representation
- Filtering lists
