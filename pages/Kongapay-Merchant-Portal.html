Kongapay Merchant Portal

Client

Kongapay helps businesses/merchents to recieve money and also give them access to a plethora of financial services. vist kongapay.com for more information

Task

I was tasked with redesigning a merchant portal ( a desktop web application ) built to display transactional reports and other meaningful statistical reports to merchants about their business.

The Goal of the project

- Revamp whole experience yo better suit customers - Improve table arrangement - improve UX for filtering list - Dssplay Infographics on the dashboard properly - Maintain consistency in different elements

Role

- Sole UX Designer
- Frontend Developer

Design Process

Using the former portal as a list of requirements, after a few sketches and wireframes, I proposed a new looks for the portal. Afterward, we proceeded to a survey with testers and a couple of the merchants. A final design was formed out of a series of reforms due to customer specifications, requests, and suggestions.

Design Process

A dark theme was chosen due to the fact that a good number of the users were elderly and they found it easier to read with a dark background.
